About Avatsi

Built around the craft of audio video, automation, and premium everyday use.

Avatsi combines residential and commercial AV thinking into systems that are refined, dependable, and genuinely pleasant to live with. The company focus spans audio, video, home automation, home theatre, speakers, HiFi, commercial AV, and meeting room solutions.

Residential Experience

From home theatre rooms and HiFi listening spaces to living rooms, bedrooms, and kitchens, Avatsi shapes technology around comfort, aesthetics, and long-term usability.

Commercial Capability

For offices and business spaces, we support commercial AV, distributed audio, and meeting room environments that need clarity, reliability, and straightforward control.

Design Approach

Every project starts with how the room should feel, then moves into hardware selection, infrastructure planning, integration, tuning, and intuitive control.
